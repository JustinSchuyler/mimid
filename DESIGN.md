# Mimid

Mimid is an app to help you prepare for interviews.

## Interview Types

Mimid focuses on technical interviews in the software engineering domain. There will be 3 interview types to start:

1. **Systems Design:** architect a scalable system.
2. **Coding:** problem-solving, maintainability, data structures and algorithms.
3. **Behavioral:** describe your experience and accomplishments.

## Interview Configurator

Each interview can be customized:

- Pick a specific question
- Choose a difficulty level
- Set the user's role to interviewee or interviewer

## Question Bank

The user may add questions to a bank, along with answers and style guides. This enables question reuse as well as steering, to help Mimid align with personal and team preferences.

## Bring Your Own Key

To start, Mimid will enable the user to bring their own API key, so they will have ownership over their calls to Claude. Mimid will ensure transparency by keeping the user informed regarding their usage.
